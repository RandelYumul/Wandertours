DISNEYLAND, HONGKONG

Discover the enchantment of Disneyland Hong Kong, where Disney’s beloved characters and stories come to life. Explore themed lands such as Adventureland and Fantasyland, each offering unique attractions and immersive experiences.

Enjoy a variety of thrilling rides, captivating shows, and character meet-and-greets. Whether you're seeking excitement or magic, Disneyland Hong Kong provides a memorable adventure for visitors of all ages.

SWITZERLAND

Experience the stunning beauty of Switzerland, a country renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and vibrant culture. From the majestic Alps to charming lakeside villages, Switzerland offers a diverse range of natural wonders and picturesque scenery.

Immerse yourself in outdoor adventures like skiing, hiking, and scenic train rides. Discover historic cities, world-class museums, and delectable Swiss cuisine, making Switzerland a perfect destination for both relaxation and exploration.

VENICE, ITALY

Discover the timeless charm of Venice, Italy’s enchanting city of canals. Wander through its labyrinthine streets, glide along picturesque canals in a gondola, and marvel at iconic landmarks such as St. Mark’s Basilica and the Rialto Bridge.

Experience the city’s rich history and vibrant culture, from its historic piazzas to its renowned art and cuisine. Venice offers a unique blend of romance, history, and beauty that captivates visitors from around the world.
